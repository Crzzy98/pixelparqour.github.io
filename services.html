Our Commitment to Your Security: Insurance Highlights

To ensure the highest level of trust and protection, Pixel Parqour LLC DBA Pixel Parqour holds a comprehensive CYBERCLEAR Cyber and Data Risk insurance policy with Hiscox Insurance Company Inc.

This policy is designed to immediately cover the costs and liability associated with protecting client data and maintaining business continuity following a cyber event.

1. Robust Financial Protection

Overall Limit of Liability: The policy provides a substantial overall limit of $250,000 to cover expenses and damages resulting from covered cyber events and digital liability claims.

Expert Response Funding: This coverage is specifically designed to pay for the immediate, specialized response required to investigate and resolve an event.

2. Comprehensive Data and Network Protection

Our policy includes critical coverage features that provide peace of mind in the event of a security failure or data breach:

Data Breach Response & Regulatory Defense: Covers damages, claim expenses, and penalties resulting from a regulatory proceeding for a consumer privacy violation. This ensures we have the resources to meet compliance requirements and defend against legal actions related to your data.

Ransomware Defense: Includes a dedicated sub-limit of $25,000 to cover loss incurred from a covered ransomware event, ensuring a rapid, effective resolution to cyber extortion threats.

Business Interruption Coverage: Provides financial support to minimize the impact of downtime caused by a system failure.

Reputation Management: Includes a $50,000 sub-limit to cover costs associated with restoring the company's and our clients' reputations following a damaging cyber event.

3. Immediate Access to Breach Experts

Following an event, the speed and expertise of the response are critical. Our policy guarantees immediate access to a professional breach team:

24/7 Breach Coach® Access: We have direct, 24/7 access to a specialized "Breach Coach®" hotline to immediately report a cyber event and launch a professional response team.

Value-Added Response Services: Our policy includes a comprehensive panel of breach response resources, featuring:

  • Legal Services and Public Relations Support to manage all communication and legal requirements.
  • Credit Monitoring and Extortion Protection services for affected individuals.
  • Access to the eRisk Hub® for best-in-class response information.

4. Digital Media Liability (Reputation and Content)

The policy also protects against claims arising from our digital media activities, covering:

Intellectual Property Claims: Damages and expenses related to alleged copyright, trademark, or content infringement.

Defamation and Privacy Violations: Coverage for claims alleging libel, slander, or invasion of privacy.

Disclaimer: This is a simplified summary for client assurance. Full terms, conditions, and exclusions are contained within the Hiscox CYBERCLEAR Policy documents.
